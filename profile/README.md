## What Is an eSIM?

An eSIM (embedded SIM) is a digital SIM card that lets you activate a mobile plan without using a physical SIM. It is fast, convenient, and perfect for travel. Instead of buying a new SIM in each country, you simply scan a QR code and activate your plan within seconds.

Most eSIM providers offer:

- International travel eSIMs  
- Country-specific and regional data plans  
- Global eSIM packages  
- Unlimited data options  
- 5G high-speed internet  
- QR-code instant activation  

eSIMs work on smartphones, tablets, wearables, and even laptops that support eSIM technology.
